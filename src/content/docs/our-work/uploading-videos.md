---
title: Uploading Videos
description: How to upload videos to the Our Work gallery — they show a play button in the grid and open in a lightbox.
---

Videos work the same way as photos on the Our Work page — upload them to the **"gallery"** folder in Directus and they appear on the website automatically. Videos show a play button icon in the gallery grid and open in a full-screen viewer when clicked.

---

## Supported Video Format

- **MP4** — the only supported video format

:::caution
Make sure your video is in **MP4 format** before uploading. Videos in other formats (MOV, AVI, MKV, etc.) will not display correctly on the website. You can convert videos to MP4 using a free online tool — ask your developer if you need help with this.
:::

---

## Step-by-Step: Uploading a Video

1. Log in to Directus.
2. Click the **Files** icon in the left sidebar.
3. Click the **"gallery"** folder.
4. Click the **Upload** button.
5. Click **Upload a File** and select your MP4 video — or drag and drop it.
6. **Wait patiently** — videos are much larger files than photos and may take a few minutes to upload depending on your internet speed.
7. Once the upload finishes, the video will appear in the gallery folder and on the website.

---

## File Size Tips

Large video files take longer to upload and may slow down your website for visitors. To keep things running fast:

- **Keep videos under 50 MB** when possible
- **Trim your videos** — shorter clips (15–60 seconds) work best for a gallery
- If you need help compressing a video, ask your developer

:::tip
Videos recorded on a modern smartphone in standard quality are usually a good size for the gallery. Avoid uploading raw 4K footage directly.
:::

---

## Video Order

Like photos, videos appear with **newest uploads first** in the gallery. If you want a specific video to appear near the top, upload it last.

---

## Removing a Video

1. Open the **Files** area and go to the **gallery** folder.
2. Find the video you want to remove.
3. Right-click it (or use the three-dot menu) and select **Delete**.
4. Confirm the deletion.

:::caution
Deleting a file is **permanent** — there is no undo. Save a copy on your computer before deleting anything.
:::
