Your website content is managed through a tool called **Directus**, your CMS (content management system), the "behind the scenes" area where you update your site.

To make any changes to your website, you start here.

---

## Logging In

![Login Page Screen Capture](/login.png)

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, or Firefox).
2. Type your **Directus login link** into the address bar and press **Enter**.
   - Your developer gave you this link when your site launched. It looks something like: `https://cms.yourwebsite.com`
3. Click the **Email** field and type your email address.
4. Click the **Password** field and type your password.
5. Click the **Sign In** button.

You'll land on your Directus dashboard, it's a panel with a menu on the left side.

![Dashboard Screen Capture](/dashboard.png)

---

## If You Forget Your Password

1. On the login screen, click **Forgot Password** (it's below or to the right of the Sign In button).
2. Type your email address and click **Reset**.
3. Check your email inbox for a message from Directus.
4. Click the link in that email and follow the steps to create a new password.

:::tip
If you don't see the reset email after a minute or two, check your **spam** or **junk** folder.
:::

---

## Staying Logged In

Directus will keep you logged in for a while, but it may log you out automatically after some time. If you return and see the login screen, just sign in again.
