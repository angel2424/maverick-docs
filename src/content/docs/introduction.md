Welcome! This is your personal guide for managing your Maverick Mobile Home Services website.

You'll find step-by-step instructions for updating every section of your site — from your phone number and business hours to photos, service descriptions, and more.

**You don't need any technical knowledge.** If you can fill out a form, you can update your website.

---

## What's in This Guide

Use the menu on the left to jump to any topic.

| Section | What you can do |
|---|---|
| **Getting Started** | Learn how to log in and find your way around |
| **Global Settings** | Update phone number, email, hours, and social media |
| **Home Page** | Edit the headline, services section, and owner's message |
| **About Page** | Update your story, experience, and values |
| **Services Pages** | Manage city-specific pages and service descriptions |
| **Our Work Gallery** | Upload photos and videos |
| **Reference** | Formatting tips and troubleshooting |

---

## Quick Reference: Common Tasks

| I want to… | Where to go |
|---|---|
| Change my phone number | Global Settings → Contact Info & Hours |
| Update business hours | Global Settings → Contact Info & Hours |
| Edit the main headline on the home page | Home Page → Hero Section |
| Add a photo to the gallery | Our Work Gallery → Uploading Photos |
| Edit a service name or description | Services Pages → Managing Services |
| Update social media links | Global Settings → Social Media Links |
| Change the footer text | Global Settings → Footer Text |
| Edit the About page story | About Page → Hero & Our Story |
| Add a new city page | Services Pages → Overview & City Pages |

---

## Before You Start

You'll need two things:

1. Your **Directus login link** — provided by your developer when your site launched.
2. Your **username and password**.

Start with [How to Log In](/getting-started/how-to-log-in) if you haven't logged in before.
