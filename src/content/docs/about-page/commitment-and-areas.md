---

The Commitment section describes your promise to customers. The Service Areas section highlights the cities and regions you serve. Both appear near the bottom of the About page.

![Commitment and Service areas Section Screen Capture](/commitment.png)

---

## Where to Find It

1. Log in to Directus.
2. Click **Content** in the left sidebar.
3. Click **about** (for English) or **about_es** (for Spanish).
4. Scroll down to find the **Commitment Section** and **Areas Section** fields.

---

## Commitment Section Fields

| Field | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Title** | The heading for the Commitment section |
| **Description** | The full commitment statement, supports markdown formatting |

### Step-by-Step: Editing the Commitment Section

1. Open **about** (or **about_es**).
2. Click the **Title** field and update the heading.
3. Click the **Description** field and update the text.
4. Click **Save**.

:::tip
The **Description** field supports markdown. You can use bold text, bullet points, and paragraph breaks to make it easier to read. See the [Formatting Guide](/reference/formatting-guide).
:::

---

## Service Areas Section Fields

| Field | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Title** | The heading for the Service Areas section (e.g., "Where We Serve") |
| **Intro Description** | The intro paragraph before the city list |
| **Outro Description** | The closing paragraph after the city list |
| **Service Area Image** | The photo displayed in this section (e.g., a map or regional photo) |

### Step-by-Step: Editing the Service Areas Section

1. Open **about** (or **about_es**).
2. Click the **Title** field and update the heading.
3. Click the **Intro Description** field and update the intro paragraph.
4. Click the **Outro Description** field and update the closing paragraph.
5. To update the photo, click **Service Area Image** and upload a new image.
6. Click **Save**.

:::note
The actual list of cities shown in this section comes from the **settings** collection (**Cities** field), not from this page. To add or remove a city from the list, see [Contact Info & Hours](/global-settings/contact-info).
:::
