The Trust section builds confidence with visitors, and the Expertise section highlights your qualifications and experience. Both appear in the middle portion of the About page.


---

## Where to Find It

1. Log in to Directus.
2. Click **Content** in the left sidebar.
3. Click **about** (for English) or **about_es** (for Spanish).
4. Scroll down to the **Trust Section** and **Expertise Section** fields.

---
![Trust Section Screen Capture](/trust.png)

## Trust Section Fields

| Field | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Title** | The heading for the Trust section |
| **Subtitle** | A secondary line of text below the heading |
| **Description** | The description paragraph — supports markdown formatting |

### Step-by-Step: Editing the Trust Section

1. Open the **about** collection.
2. Click the **Title** field and update the heading.
3. Click the **Subtitle** field and update the secondary text.
4. Click the **Description** field and update the paragraph.
5. Click **Save**.

:::tip
The Trust section is a great place to mention any licenses, certifications, or guarantees your business offers.
:::

## Expertise Section Fields

| Field | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Title** | The heading for the Expertise section |
| **Subtitle** | A secondary line of text below the heading |
| **Description** | The main description paragraph |
| **Expertise Image** | The photo displayed alongside the expertise content |
| **Experience List** | A bulleted list of experience highlights — supports markdown formatting |

### Step-by-Step: Editing the Expertise Section

1. Open the **about** collection.
2. Click the **Title** field and update the heading.
3. Click the **Subtitle** field and update the secondary text.
4. Click the **Description** field and update the paragraph.
5. To update the photo, click **Expertise Image** and upload a new image.
6. Click the **Experience List** field and update the list of highlights.
7. Click **Save**.

---

## Formatting the Experience List

The **Experience List** field uses markdown. To create a bullet list, start each line with `- `:

```
- 25+ years of hands-on experience
- Licensed and insured in Texas
- Serving Eagle Pass and surrounding areas since 1998
- Specialized in leveling, plumbing, and electrical repairs
```

For the full markdown reference, see the [Formatting Guide](/reference/formatting-guide).
