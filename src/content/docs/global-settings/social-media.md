---
title: Social Media Links
description: How to add, update, or remove your social media links.
---

Your social media links appear as icons in the footer of every page. This guide shows you how to update them.

---

## Where to Find It

1. Log in to Directus.
2. Click **Content** in the left sidebar.
3. Click **settings**.
4. Scroll down to the **Social Media Links** field.

---

## Editing an Existing Link

1. Click on the social media entry you want to update.
2. Click the **url** field and replace it with the correct link to your profile.
   - Example: `https://www.facebook.com/yourbusiness`
3. Click **Save** in the top right corner.

---

## Adding a New Social Media Account

1. In the **Social Media Links** area, click **+ Create New**.
2. Fill in the following fields:
   - **name** — The platform name (e.g., `Facebook`, `Instagram`, `YouTube`)
   - **url** — The full link to your profile page
   - **icon** — The icon name for that platform (ask your developer if you're unsure)
3. Click **Save**.

:::note
If you're not sure which icon to use for a new platform, leave the icon field blank and send a note to your developer — they can set it for you.
:::

---

## Removing a Social Media Account

1. In the **Social Media Links** field, click the entry you want to remove.
2. Click the **Delete** icon (trash can).
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
4. Click **Save** on the main settings page.
