The footer of your website has three text areas: a heading, an intro sentence, and quote details. All three can be updated in both English and Spanish.

![Footer Section Screen Capture](/footer.png)

---

## Where to Find It

1. Log in to Directus.
2. Click **Content** in the left sidebar.
3. Click **settings**.
4. Scroll down to the **Translations** section.
5. Click the **English** tab to edit English text, or the **Spanish** tab for Spanish.

![Trust Section Screen Capture](/footer-translation.png)

---

## Field Reference

| Field | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Title** | The bold heading at the top of the footer (e.g., "Let's get started") |
| **Description** | The intro sentence below the heading |
| **Quote Details** | The details a customer needs to provide before asking for a quote at the bottom of the footer. Supports markdown formatting |

---

## Step-by-Step: Updating Footer Text

1. Open the **settings** collection.
2. Scroll down to the **Translations** section and click the **English** tab.
3. Click the **Title** field and type your new heading.
4. Click the **Description** field and update the intro sentence.
5. Click the **Quote Details** field and update the closing message.
6. Click the **Spanish** tab and repeat the same steps for the Spanish version.
7. Click **Save** when done.

---

## Formatting the Closing Quote

The **Quote Details** field supports **markdown** — a simple way to add formatting to text.

| To do this… | Type this… |
|---|---|
| **Bold text** | `**your text here**` |
| *Italic text* | `*your text here*` |
| New paragraph | Leave a blank line between paragraphs |

:::tip
Keep the closing quote short and memorable — one or two sentences works best.
:::

For the full formatting reference, see the [Formatting Guide](/reference/formatting-guide).
