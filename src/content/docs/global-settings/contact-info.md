This page controls your contact information, logo, and business hours, things that appear in the header and footer of **every page** on your website. If your phone number changes, this is where you update it.

---

## Where to Find It

1. Log in to Directus.
2. Click **Content** in the left sidebar.
3. Click **settings**.
4. The settings item opens directly (there is only one, no list to choose from).

![Footer Screen Capture](/settings-business.png)

---

## Step-by-Step: Updating Contact Info

1. Click the **Phone Number** field and type your new phone number.
2. Click the **Email** field and type your new email address.
3. Click the **Business Hours** field and type your current hours (e.g., `Mon–Fri: 8am–6pm, Sat: 9am–3pm`).
4. Click **Save** in the top right corner.

---

## Field Reference

| Field | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Colored Logo** | The logo that appears in the website header. Upload a new image here to change it. |
| **White Logo** | The white version of your logo shown in the footer. |
| **OG Image** | The image that appears when someone shares your website link on Facebook, WhatsApp, or other social media. |
| **Email** | Your contact email address, shown in the footer. |
| **Phone Number** | Your phone number, shown in the footer and contact areas. |
| **Business Hours** | Your hours of operation, shown in the footer. |
| **Social Media Links** | Your social media links. See [Social Media Links](/global-settings/social-media). |
| **Cities** | The list of cities you serve. See the section below. |

---

## Updating Your Logo

1. Click the **Colored Logo** field.
2. Click **Upload a File** (or drag and drop your new logo image onto the field).
3. Wait for the upload to complete.
4. Repeat for **White Logo** if you also want to update the footer version.
5. Click **Save**.

:::tip
For the best results, use a **PNG or SVG file** with a transparent background for your logos.
:::

---

## Managing Service Cities

The **Cities** field lists every city that appears in your navigation and services pages.

:::caution
Each city has a **slug**, a short version of the city name used in the website URL. For example, the city `Eagle Pass` should be written as `eagle-pass`. Then on your website, the city's service page appears as `yoursite.com/services/eagle-pass`. Once you add a city in this section, go ahead and add the service page and the relevant description to the service list.
:::

To **update a city name** (the display name only, not the slug):
1. Click the **Cities** field.
2. Click the city entry you want to update.
3. Edit the name.
4. Click **Save**.
