The expertise section highlights your company's depth of knowledge and experience. It includes a label, a heading, a short description, and a photo.

![Expertise Section Screen Capture](/expertise.png)

---

## Where to Find It

1. Log in to Directus.
2. Click **Content** in the left sidebar.
3. Click **Pages (English) > Home** (for English) or **Pages (Spanish) > Home** (for Spanish).
4. Scroll down to the **Expertise Section** fields.

---

## Field Reference

| Field | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Subtitle** | The small uppercase label above the heading |
| **Title** | The section heading (e.g., "25+ Years of Experience") |
| **Description** | The paragraph of text describing your expertise |
| **Image** | The photo displayed alongside the text |

---

## Step-by-Step: Editing the Expertise Section

1. Open **home** (or **home_es** for Spanish).
2. Click the **Subtitle** field and update the label.
3. Click the **Title** field and update the heading.
   - You can use `*asterisks*` around a word to make it appear in amber: e.g., `*25+ Years* of Experience`
4. Click the **Description** field and update the description.
5. To update the photo, click the **Image** field and upload a new image.
6. Click **Save**.

---

## Changing the Photo

1. Click the **Image** field.
2. Click **Upload a File** (or drag and drop a photo from your computer).
3. Wait for the upload to complete.
4. Click **Save**.

:::tip
Use a high-quality, well-lit photo. Recommended size: at least **800×600 pixels**. Avoid blurry or dark photos — they make the section look unprofessional.
:::
