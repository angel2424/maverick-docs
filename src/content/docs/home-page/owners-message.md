The Owner's Message section is a personal note from you to your customers, a quote or short statement that builds trust. It appears near the bottom of the home page, and it includes your message and a call-to-action button.

![Message Section Screen Capture](/home-message.png)

---

## Where to Find It

1. Log in to Directus.
2. Click **Content** in the left sidebar.
3. Click **Pages (English) > Home** (for English) or **Pages (Spanish) > Home** (for Spanish).
4. Scroll down to the **Owner's Message** fields.

---

## Field Reference

| Field | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Message** | Your personal quote or message to customers |
| **Action Button** | The call-to-action button below your message |

---

## Step-by-Step: Updating Your Message

1. Open **home** (or **home_es** for Spanish).
2. Click the **Message** field.
3. Type your message. Write it in your own voice, as if you're speaking directly to a customer.
4. Click **Save**.

---

## Tips for a Great Owner's Message

- **Keep it personal:** use "I" and "we." This is your voice, not a corporate statement.
- **Keep it short:** 2 to 4 sentences is ideal. One powerful paragraph goes further than a long one.
- **Focus on trust:** mention your experience, your commitment, or your community roots.

:::tip
**Example:** *"With over 25 years serving Eagle Pass and the surrounding area, my team and I take pride in treating every home like it's our own. When you call Maverick, you're calling a neighbor."*
:::
